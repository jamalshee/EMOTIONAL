What does this mean?

According to research conducted by Six Seconds (a non-profit organization dedicated to supporting people to create positive change), Emotional Intelligence is strongly linked to increased performance outcome scores of Effectiveness, Relationships, Wellbeing & Quality of Life. That’s one reason the World Economic Forum identifies it as one of the essential skills for the future (6sec.org/wef).
